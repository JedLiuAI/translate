# Translation Checklist

Use this checklist before finalizing any translated medicine leaflet PDF.

## Clinical fidelity

- Drug name is translated consistently.
- Strength per dose is unchanged.
- Dose counts and administration frequency are unchanged.
- Timing such as "15 minutes before exercise" is unchanged.
- Contraindications are not softened or broadened.
- Pregnancy, lactation, pediatric, athlete, and overdose warnings are preserved.
- Storage temperature and handling warnings are preserved.

## OCR risk handling

- Ignore obviously broken OCR fragments unless they change medical meaning.
- Reconstruct expected headings from context only when highly confident.
- Keep uncertain company-address details brief if the scan is poor.
- State in the final response that scan-based OCR should be manually reviewed before formal use.

## Layout

- Keep the front/back panel structure.
- Keep the overall information hierarchy close to the source.
- Prefer readable Chinese text over exact typography matching.
- If text overflows, shrink body font or tighten line spacing before dropping clinically relevant content.
